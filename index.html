About

Welcome!

Ashleigh is a Software Engineer and Designer passionate about making fun games. She is a Gameplay Engineer for the virtual world MMOG Kaneva as well as a highly involved member of her local game development community.

Prior to joining Kaneva, she acquired her passion for game development through her former role of Vice President for Georgia Southern University's Aurora Game Development Club.
